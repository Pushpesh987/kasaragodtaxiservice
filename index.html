Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our taxi and tour services in Kasaragod.

What is the best taxi service in Kasaragod? expand_more

Sahas Tours Kasaragod (Kasaragod Taxi Services) is the most trusted taxi service in Kasaragod, operating since 1975. We are registered with the All Kerala Taxi Union and offer 24/7 cab booking, airport transfers, tour packages, and outstation travel. Our fleet includes sedans, SUVs, tempo travellers, and buses.

How do I book a taxi from Kasaragod to Mangalore Airport? expand_more

You can book a Kasaragod to Mangalore Airport taxi by calling +91 95267 62257 or messaging us on WhatsApp. We offer punctual airport pickup and drop services with comfortable sedans and SUVs. We also serve Kannur International Airport (CNN).

Do you offer tour packages from Kasaragod? expand_more

Yes! We offer customized tour packages to Munnar, Wayanad, Ooty, Coorg, Bekal Fort, Kerala backwaters, and pilgrimage destinations like Mookambika, Dharmasthala, and Udupi. Packages are available for families, honeymoon couples, corporate groups, and solo travelers.

What types of vehicles are available for hire in Kasaragod? expand_more

We offer sedans (Swift Dzire, Toyota Etios), SUVs (Toyota Innova Crysta, Marazzo), Tempo Travellers (12-17 seater), minibuses, and luxury coaches. All vehicles are well-maintained, air-conditioned, and driven by experienced professional drivers.

Is Kasaragod Taxi Services available 24/7? expand_more

Yes, Kasaragod Taxi Services (Sahas Tours) operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Whether it's an early morning airport transfer or a late-night emergency, we are always available. Call +91 95267 62257 anytime.

Can I book a taxi from Kasaragod to Bangalore or other cities? expand_more

Absolutely! We provide outstation taxi services from Kasaragod to Bangalore, Mysore, Mangalore, Kochi, Coimbatore, and many other cities across Kerala, Karnataka, and Tamil Nadu. Our experienced drivers ensure safe and comfortable long-distance travel.
